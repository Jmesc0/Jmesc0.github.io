I’ve been replaying a lot of the older pokemon games in my free time, And I didnt have any ideas of what to draw at the time, so I decided to draw the team I used in my HeartGold / SoulSilver playthrough. This was the team I ended up beating the Elite 4 with, which is usually the end of the game, say for any post game. It was really fun using my quicker, “sketchy” style again, and I personally really liked how it looked, and it also didn't take forever to draw which is something I tend to have an issue with. Drawing the pokemon was also fun and fast. I did really like it and I’m probably going to do more E4 / pokemon stuff if I have nothing to think of.
